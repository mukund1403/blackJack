How to Play

Place your bet before clicking start game

You can win the game, either by having a total score higher than the dealer's or getting exactly 21

If your score is higher than the dealers, you will win your bet amount. If it is 21, you win double your bet amount

If the sum of your cards go over 21 you will be bust and lose the game!

If the sum is below 21, you can choose to draw a third card or stay

Once you choose to stay it will be the dealers turn

Jack, King and Queen are all 10 points while Ace is 11
